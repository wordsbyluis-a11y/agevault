Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2025

The short version: We do not collect or store your name. Your email address is used exclusively to deliver your purchased report. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to any third party.

1. Who We Are

AgeVault ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at agevault.com. We provide a precision age-calculation tool and optional paid life reports.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to provide our service:

  • Date of birth — entered by you in the calculator form. This is processed in your browser and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.
  • Email address — collected at the time of calculation and used solely to deliver your purchased report. We do not store email addresses beyond the delivery period.

We explicitly do not collect names. The calculator does not ask for, transmit, or store any name-related information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate and display your age statistics (processed entirely client-side, in your browser).
  • To deliver your purchased age report to the email address you provided.
  • To pre-fill your email address on the Stripe payment checkout page for your convenience.

4. Payments

All payment processing is handled by Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. We do not receive, process, or store your credit card details. Stripe's own privacy policy governs how your payment data is handled. You can review it at stripe.com/privacy.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregate traffic patterns. These tools do not identify you personally. We use Google AdSense for advertising, which may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out via Google's ad settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Email delivery providers (solely to send your report)

7. Data Retention

Your date of birth is never stored on our servers — all calculation happens in your browser. Your email address is retained only as long as needed to deliver your report, and is deleted thereafter.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or similar laws, including the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@agevault.com.

9. Security

Our site uses SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS) for all connections. Payment data is protected by Stripe's 256-bit encryption. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any data we process.

10. Children's Privacy

AgeVault is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Significant changes will be noted by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at contact@agevault.com.
